    • Details and Financials - ID Institution Directory (ID).Use more criteria to, Find FDIC Insured Banks and their locations, Get comprehensive financial or demographic reports, Get current and historical data, Find groups of banks, Find a bank holding company (BHC).
    • Branch Office Deposits Summary of Deposits (SOD). Results of the annual survey of branch office deposits as of June 30th for all FDIC-insured institutions. Data is available by institution and/or geographic area in reports or data downloads
    • Securities Exchange Act Filings Securities Exchange Act Filings. This system contains two groups of securities disclosure filings: Beneficial ownership report filings on Forms 3, 4, and 5 by directors, officers, and principal shareholders of depository institutions. Other securities disclosure documents filed by or pertaining to FDIC-supervised depository institutions with a class of securities registered under the Exchange Act.
  • Reports & Analysis Summary of Deposits (SOD). Based on the annual survey of branch office deposits as of June 30th, market share reports are available by geographic area (Deposit Market Share Report, Pro Forma HHI) or by institution (Market Presence, Growth Rates).
    • Deposit Market Share Reports Summary of Deposits (SOD). Based on the annual survey of branch office deposits as of June 30th, market share reports are available by geographic area (Deposit Market Share Report, Pro Forma HHI) or by institution (Market Presence, Growth Rates).
    • Comparison Reports - SDI Statistics on Depository Institutions (SDI). Financial data from 1992 to present, Compare up to four columns. Create custom peers, reports, and downloads. Predefined industry reports.
    • Standard Industry Reports - SDI Statistics on Depository Institutions (SDI). Detailed aggregate financial information for common standard peer groups. Based on legacy Statistics on Banking (SOB) reports.
    • Bank Failures Historical Statistics on Banking (HSOB). A complete look historically of bank failures and assistance transactions of all FDIC-insured institutions back to 1934.
    • Reports of Structure Changes Reports of Structure Changes (ROC). Searchable database for structure transactions (e.g., new institution, mergers, closings) for institutions or offices for a specific date, date range, or quarter date based on either the processed date or effective date.
  • Reference Tables Summary of Deposits (SOD). Standardized tables provide aggregate results of the annual survey of branch office deposits (SOD) as of June 30th. National, regional, state and county data are available for data back to 1994.
    • Deposit Summary Tables Summary of Deposits (SOD). Standardized tables provide aggregate results of the annual survey of branch office deposits (SOD) as of June 30th. National, regional, state and county data are available for data back to 1994.
    • Annual Financial Data Historical Statistics on Banking (HSOB). Annual summary of financial and structural data back to 1934 for all FDIC-insured institutions. These data can be used to identify and analyze long-term trends and to develop benchmarks to evaluate the current condition of the banking and thrift industries. Commercial bank data back to 1934. Savings institution data back to 1984.
  • Data Download Statistics on Depository Institutions (SDI). Quarterly financial data for all FDIC-Insured institutions. Predefined downloadable ZIP files contains all SDI Reports and report items. Each ZIP file contains data for one reporting quarter.
    • Quarterly Financial Data - SDI Statistics on Depository Institutions (SDI). Quarterly financial data for all FDIC-Insured institutions. Predefined downloadable ZIP files contains all SDI Reports and report items. Each ZIP file contains data for one reporting quarter.
    • Custom Financial Data - SDI Statistics on Depository Institutions (SDI). Quarterly financial data for all FDIC-Insured institutions. Select report items from any SDI Report. Select one or many FDIC Insured Institutions. Select time periods 1992 to present.
    • Institutions & Locations - ID Institution Directory (ID). Predefined datasets for all FDIC-insured institutions (Updated weekly): Current list of all institutions. Current locations for all institutions (branches and main offices).
    • Financial Data - SDI Predefined datasets for all FDIC-insured institutions. Each of the downloadable ZIP files contains all financial items available for all FDIC insured institutions. Each ZIP file contains data for one reporting quarter.
    • Aggregate Time Series Data Quarterly Banking Profile (QBP)Time Series Spreadsheets. Aggregate data for all FDIC-insured institutions for each quarter back to 1984 in downloadable Excel spreadsheet files.
    • Branch Office Deposits Branch Office Deposits. Annual Summary of Deposits survey of branch office deposits as of June 30. Data is available back to 1994.
